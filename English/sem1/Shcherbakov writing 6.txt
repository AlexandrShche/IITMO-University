I want to tell you about my father.
He was born in 1960 in a small town in the Krasnoyarsk Territory.
Then his family moved to China.
He started school in 1966. And in 1976 he finished scholl.
He graduated from the railway technical school.
From 1978 to 1980 he served in the Soviet Army.
Then my father worked as a machinist's assistant until he was forced to leave his job for health reasons
In 1996, he began to receive higher education and in 2001 received a diploma.
Until 2019, he worked as an engineer. 
Today he works with network technologies and telecommunications.
